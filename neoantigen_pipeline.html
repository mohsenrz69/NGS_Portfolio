🧬

Cancer neoantigen prediction pipeline

Goal and advantage of each step — from patient samples to a personalised mRNA cancer vaccine.

Input
🔬
Tumor whole-exome sequencing
Captures all coding mutations Cost-effective vs whole genome
🧪
Matched normal sequencing
Separates somatic from germline Eliminates false positives
🔭
Tumor RNA sequencing
Confirms gene expression Detects splicing neoantigens
QC / Align
✅
Quality control & read trimming
Removes low-quality bases Prevents downstream errors
📍
Reference genome alignment
Maps reads to genomic positions Enables precise variant detection
📊
Expression quantification
Measures transcript abundance Filters unexpressed variants
Variant call
🎯
Somatic mutation detection
Identifies tumor-specific changes SNVs, indels, frameshifts
📈
Tumor purity & copy number
Corrects allele frequency estimates Improves clonality assessment
🧹
Variant filtering
Removes germline noise Retains high-confidence calls
HLA
🔑
Patient-specific HLA allele determination
Enables personalized binding prediction Covers class I and class II alleles High-resolution 4-digit typing Critical — wrong HLA = wrong predictions
Annotate
🏷️
Functional variant annotation
Links variants to protein changes Filters synonymous mutations Adds population frequency context
🌡️
Expression-based filtering
Confirms variant is actually expressed Reduces non-immunogenic candidates
⚗️
Mutant peptide generation
Produces all candidate neoepitopes Multiple peptide lengths for MHC coverage
MHC bind
🧲
MHC class I binding prediction
Identifies peptides presented to CD8⁺ T cells Core filter for immunogenicity potential
🤝
MHC class II binding prediction
Identifies peptides presented to CD4⁺ T cells Enhances vaccine breadth & durability
🎚️
Affinity-based candidate selection
Drastically reduces candidate space Prioritises strong binders
Structure new
🧊
Peptide–MHC 3D complex modelling
Validates binding pose in the groove Reveals peptide conformation
🔗
TCR–pMHC docking simulation
Predicts actual T-cell engagement Beyond binding affinity alone
⚖️
Structural stability scoring
Thermodynamic confirmation of binding H-bond network & contact analysis
📌
Anchor residue & TCR contact analysis
Confirms exposed immunogenic residues Guides peptide design optimisation
Rank
⚡
T-cell immunogenicity scoring
Predicts likelihood of immune activation Goes beyond binding prediction
🌀
Clonality & cancer cell fraction
Prioritises clonal mutations Present in all tumor cells
🏆
Integrated neoantigen ranking
Combines all scores into one rank Structural score improves precision Produces a final candidate shortlist
Output
💉
Personalised mRNA cancer vaccine
Encodes only patient-specific neoantigens Targets mutations absent in healthy tissue Rapidly manufacturable per patient Activates both CD8⁺ and CD4⁺ T-cell responses Structurally validated candidates improve efficacy
