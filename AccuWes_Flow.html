↑ Higher precision through caller agreement
↑ CNVs detected — a major missed disease cause in standard pipelines
↑ Parallel execution keeps runtime equivalent
🔬
Stage 6
Population-Aware Filtering
Filtering thresholds adapt to the patient's ethnic background, avoiding mislabeling population-common variants as pathogenic
↑ Fewer false positives in non-European patients
📚
Stage 7
Broader Biological Context
Cross-references clinical, global, and region-specific population frequency databases — including dedicated Iranian and Middle Eastern resources — reducing false pathogenicity calls in under-represented populations
↑ Covers Iranian ethnic diversity and GCC populations
↑ Fewer false positives in patients missed by global databases
⚕️
Stage 8
Phenotype-Driven Classification
Patient symptoms guide variant ranking — reviewers see a short prioritized list instead of hundreds of unranked candidates. Classifications update automatically as new evidence emerges.
↑ Dramatically lower VUS burden
↑ Patients can receive a diagnosis later without re-sequencing
